Unique meeting rooms with excellent service


Definitely one of the most trendy meeting locations of Noordwijk. A heartbeat away from the beach and the centre of Noordwijk is easily accessible. Vesper offers 3 different unique meeting rooms, each with its own identity. The Parlor, The Living and The Loft are particularly suitable for meetings or other business gatherings. We offer an all-inclusive price given in menu coffee and tea, mineral water, soft drinks, snacks and a two course lunch.

All three exclusive meeting rooms are well equipped with various equipment and supplies, such as: a projector, Wi-Fi, HD Smart TV, various office supplies, screen and / or flip chart. Here you can meet, have lunch and dinner.

It goes without saying that you can also have lunch on our terrace, bar or lobby. Another possibility is to go for lunch at restaurant Het Zuiderbad across the street or down at the beach at Beachclub Bries in summer season.

The Loft
